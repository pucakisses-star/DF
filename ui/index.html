WC3 Object Porter

port custom units, items, abilities & more between maps — without ever writing into your map

1  Sources

Add as many as you like — or just drag & drop maps, campaigns and asset folders anywhere in the window (drop onto the Target card to set the target). Click an object to see its model, icon and details on the right. Shift-click a checkbox to also tick everything it references (abilities, buffs, upgrades, items…).

2  Target map or campaign

(the map the objects will go into)
Optional but recommended — it lets the porter detect and fix ID collisions with objects your map already has.
